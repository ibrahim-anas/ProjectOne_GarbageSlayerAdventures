Game Name:
	Garbage Slayer Adventures

How To Play:
Use W, A, S, D to move.
Use mouse to aim and attack.

Click "Play" to begin.

Left click to shoot the water gun. The water gun can only destroy fire monsters.
Right click to attack with the broom. The broom can only destroy litter monsters and litter piles.

If there are more than 10 litter piles in the room, your health will slowly decrease.
To win, destory all the monsters and litter piles before your health runs out.

Cheat Codes:
Alt + H: Resets heath to be full
Alt + Z: Increases movement speed
Alt + F: Increases firerate of the water gun
P: Goes straight to the highscore
